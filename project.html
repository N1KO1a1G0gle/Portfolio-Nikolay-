Product design — 2026

Rebuilding a banking app's onboarding

A case study on cutting drop-off in half by rethinking identity verification as a conversation, not a form.

Role
Product design lead
Timeline
6 months
Tools
Figma, Framer
cover image

The problem

More than half of new users abandoned identity verification before finishing sign-up. The flow read like a compliance form rather than a first impression — long, unexplained, and easy to walk away from.

process sketch / flow diagram

The approach

We reframed verification as a short guided conversation, explaining why each step mattered before asking for it, and moved the riskiest step — document capture — to the point where trust was highest.

screen — before
screen — after

The result

Drop-off fell from 54% to 23% within the first quarter, and support tickets related to onboarding dropped by a third.
